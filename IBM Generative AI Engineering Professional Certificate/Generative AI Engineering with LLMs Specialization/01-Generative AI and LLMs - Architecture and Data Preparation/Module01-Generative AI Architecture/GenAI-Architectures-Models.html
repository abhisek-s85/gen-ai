03 Generative AI + Reinforcement Learning

How RL Connects to Generative Models

Reinforcement Learning (RL) trains agents to maximize a reward signal by learning which behaviors lead to better outcomes.

Generative AI models can leverage RL-style techniques during training to fine-tune their outputs toward preferred, human-aligned behaviors ‚Äî this is the core idea behind RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) workflows used to align models like GPT.

Key point: RL does not replace pre-training. It is applied after pre-training to steer the model toward desirable outputs via reward feedback.

Scenario: Text ‚Üí Personalized Video Clips

From the lesson's model set: GANs are explicitly cited for image/video generation.
In modern systems, Diffusion models are also heavily used for text-conditioned visual generation and are increasingly extended to video synthesis.
